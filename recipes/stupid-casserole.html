Stupid Casserole recipe

This casserole recipe is perfect for beginners who are new to cooking. More experienced cooks will also love how easy it is to put this casserole together!

For one of the absolute easiest ways to cook eggplant, go with this recipe for Stupid Casserole. With only 5 main ingredients, this recipe can teach anyone how to prepare eggplant. Plus, with a flavored breadcrumb and gooey cheese, this recipe can get anyone (even the kids!) interested in eating healthy foods. We called this casserole "stupid" only because it's the easiest casserole we've ever made. Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant, or 2 medium eggplants
  • 1 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup skim milk, scalded
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 cup reduced fat cheddar cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

stupid Instructions:

  1. Cut eggplant in cubes and cook in boiling water for 20 minutes until soft
  2. Afterwards, drain and salt eggplant, and then put in baking dish
  3. Add breadcrumbs, milk, onion, and cheese, salt and pepper. Mix together
  4. Cook at 325 degrees F for 30 minutes
